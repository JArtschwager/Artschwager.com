About Me

Throughout my career, I have worked as a program manager in several industries. Each position shared the need for a software that would keep the department organized and efficient for both employees and patrons.

Shortly after joining the animal welfare industry I began an apprenticeship program to train managers from across the country on how to best use technology to manage hundreds of people reporting to a single employee. Teaching managers how to use their databases to their full potential allowed me to learn the weaknesses and strengths in each database management systems.

This experience is what started my interest in coding. I wanted to find solutions to problems that developers may not be aware there is a need for. Combining 10 years of experience as a user before diving into the code behind the scenes gives me a great perspective for planning the user's experience. Being a developer has combined my bachelors in visual arts management with my personal need to problem solve.

I find I'm happiest on days I am 80% confident in what I'm doing and 100% sure there must be a better way.


To begin this journey I joined the Rutgers full stack web developer certificate program. Every day I continue to expand my programming knowledge in an industry that provides a lifetime of learning.
