About Me

Throughout my career, I have worked as a program manager in a wide range of industries. Each position shared the need for a software that would keep the department organized and efficient for both employees and patrons.

Shortly after joining the animal welfare industry I began an apprenticeship program to train managers from across the country on how to best use technology to manage hundreds of people reporting to one employee. Teaching managers how to use their databases to their best abilities allowed me to learn the weaknesses and strengths in each.

This experience is what started my interest in coding. I wanted to find solutions to problems that developers may not be aware there is a need for. Combining 10 years of experience as a user before diving into the code behind the scenes gives me a great perspective for planning for the users experience. Being a developer has combined my bachelors in visual arts management with my need to problem solve that had the chance to flourish in the Rutgers full stack web developer certificate program. I am looking forward to continuing to grow within web development and expand my programming knowledge in an industry that will never stop growing.
